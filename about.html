About The Bond

Our story, purpose, and faith foundation

Our History

The Bond was founded in 2015 by a small group of believers who recognized the need for intentional discipleship in the modern church. What began as a weekly Bible study in a living room has grown into a thriving community of over 200 members.

Our name comes from Colossians 3:14 - "And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity." We believe that Christ is the bond that unites us as believers.

"Therefore, as you received Christ Jesus the Lord, so walk in him, rooted and built up in him and established in the faith, just as you were taught, abounding in thanksgiving."

- Colossians 2:6-7

Our Purpose

Biblical Foundation

We are committed to teaching the whole counsel of God's Word, helping believers develop a biblical worldview that transforms every area of life.

Authentic Community

We create spaces for genuine relationships where members can share life, bear burdens, and encourage one another in Christ.

Intentional Discipleship

Our structured discipleship process helps believers grow from spiritual infancy to maturity, equipping them to disciple others.

Kingdom Impact

We train and send out disciples to make an impact in their families, workplaces, and communities for the glory of God.

Our Discipleship Philosophy

Biblical Foundation

We believe discipleship must be rooted in Scripture. Our teaching emphasizes understanding and applying God's Word to all areas of life.

Multiplication

Our goal is not just to make disciples, but to make disciple-makers who can reproduce the process in others.

Relational

Discipleship happens best in the context of authentic relationships where life is shared and modeled.

Transformational

We aim for heart-level change that produces Christlike character and obedience, not just head knowledge.
