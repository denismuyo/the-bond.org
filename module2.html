Introduction to Identity & the Kingdom

This lesson exposes the believer’s identity in Christ and our role as citizens of the Kingdom of God. It provides a foundation for purpose, confidence, and spiritual authority.

Matthew 4:17 (NIV): "From that time Jesus began to preach, 'Repent, for the kingdom of heaven has come near.'"

What Is Identity?

Identity is how a person defines themselves, including their characteristics, values, and relationships.

Side Note: Discuss identity crises in small groups.

Sources of Human Identity

  • Physical: Birth, gender, age, appearance
  • Soul/Personality: Traits, preferences
  • Culture & Ethnicity: Name, ancestry, background
  • Relationships: Family, friends, mentors
  • Profession: Career journey
  • Abilities: Skills, gifts
  • Religion & Beliefs: Faith source

Focus: Identity in Christ Jesus.

Identity Through Knowledge

Hosea 4:6 (NIV): "My people are destroyed from lack of knowledge."
  • Knowledge: Learning facts (Proverbs 1:7)
  • Understanding: Interpreting information (Psalm 119:34)
  • Wisdom: Applying knowledge (James 1:5, Proverbs 4:7)

Where Believers Get True Knowledge

  • God’s Word: Matthew 4:4 (NIV)
  • Spiritual mentors: 1 Corinthians 12:28 (NIV)
  • Faith cultivated through Scripture

What Is the Kingdom of God?

The Kingdom is God’s rule and influence on earth. It calls for obedience, holiness, and alignment with His will.

Christ the King & Kingdom Citizens

  • Christ is King – Revelation 19:16 (NIV)
  • Servant kingship and sacrifice – John 13:8 (NIV)
  • Victory through resurrection – Philippians 2:9-11 (NIV)

Nature of the Kingdom

  • Spiritual – Luke 17:21 (NIV)
  • Growing – Matthew 13:31-32 (NIV)
  • Inclusive – Matthew 28 (NIV)
  • Counter-cultural – “last shall be first” (Matthew 5 & 20)

Kingdom Parables

  • Good Samaritan – Luke 10:25-37
  • Prodigal Son – Luke 15:11-32
  • Sower and Seed – Matthew 13:1-23
  • Pearl of Great Value – Matthew 13:47-50

Living as Kingdom Citizens

  • Love your enemies – Matthew 5:44 (NIV)
  • Seek justice, mercy, humility – Micah 6:8 (NIV)
  • Live holy – 1 Peter 1:16 (NIV)
  • Be salt and light – Matthew 5:13-16 (NIV)
  • Seek first the Kingdom – Matthew 6:33 (NIV)
