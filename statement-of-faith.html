1. The Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, revealing His will and truth for salvation, discipleship, and godly living (2 Timothy 3:16–17).

2. God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, equal in power and glory (Matthew 28:19).

3. Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, who lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, and will return in glory (John 1:1–14; 1 Corinthians 15:3–4).

4. Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works, and results in a transformed life of obedience and fruitfulness (Ephesians 2:8–9).

5. The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells believers, empowers them for godly living, spiritual growth, and effective witness, and produces spiritual fruit (Romans 8:14; Galatians 5:22–23).

6. The Church & Discipleship

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, make disciples, live in community, and advance God’s Kingdom on earth (Matthew 28:18–20; Acts 2:42).
